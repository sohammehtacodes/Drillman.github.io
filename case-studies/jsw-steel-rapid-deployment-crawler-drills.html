JSW Steel — Rapid Deployment of Crawler Drills

Overview: JSW Steel required quick ramp-up at an iron ore site with strict uptime SLAs.

Challenge: Lead time for rigs and operators, and frequent delays waiting for spares.

Solution: Delivered crawler drills with pre-configured tooling; on-site commissioning and operator training; local spares pack staged nearby.

Outcome: Rigs commissioned in under 7 days; 12% improvement in drilling rate; unplanned stoppages reduced by 30%.

Testimonial: “Your team had our rigs cutting rock within a week. The training made a real difference.” — Operations Head, JSW Steel
