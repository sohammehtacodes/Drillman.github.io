Underground Mines, Maharashtra — Compact Drills

Overview: Tight drifts required compact, maneuverable drilling solutions.

Challenge: Conventional rigs couldn’t navigate corners and low backs; frequent repositioning.

Solution: Deployed compact drills with modular feeds; custom hose routing to avoid snags.

Outcome: Cycle time per round reduced by 17%; fewer equipment strikes in audits.

Testimonial: “Compact setups made headings far easier.” — UG Foreman, Maharashtra
