SP Coal Mines, Jharkhand — Doubling Hammer Life

Overview: Surface coal mine with highly siliceous bands causing accelerated tool wear.

Challenge: Frequent hammer rebuilds and bit damage increased cost per meter.

Solution: Introduced wear-resistant metallurgy and adjusted flushing for silica management.

Outcome: Hammer life nearly doubled; cost per meter reduced by ~22%.

Testimonial: “Consumables last much longer now.” — Maintenance Lead, SP Coal
