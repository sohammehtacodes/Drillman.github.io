Infrastructure Projects — Safer Jackhammers

Overview: Civil contractors required higher output while meeting worker safety norms.

Challenge: Hand–arm vibration and fatigue from continuous jackhammer use.

Solution: Provided jackhammers with anti‑vibration handles; toolbox safety talks and PPE pairing.

Outcome: Incidents linked to HAVS risk dropped; productivity improved due to lower fatigue.

Testimonial: “Operators can run longer without discomfort.” — Contractor HSE Officer
