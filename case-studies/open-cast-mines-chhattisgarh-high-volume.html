Open-Cast Mines, Chhattisgarh — High Volume

Overview: High production targets demanded consistent meters-per-shift across benches.

Challenge: Air supply variability and bit selection mismatches slowed progress.

Solution: Standardized compressor settings, matched bit designs to geology, and trained crews.

Outcome: Meters-per-shift rose 16% with less variation; night-shift parity achieved.

Testimonial: “Consistent output across benches at last.” — Production Manager, Chhattisgarh
