MP Mining Contractors — Faster Spare Part Delivery

Overview: Multiple sites in Madhya Pradesh struggled with delays for critical spares.

Challenge: Downtime while waiting for valves, shanks, and couplings; no buffer stock.

Solution: Set up a satellite spares hub and automated reorder triggers based on hours-run.

Outcome: Mean Time To Repair down 35%; inventory carrying cost optimized by 18%.

Testimonial: “The spares hub cut days off our repairs.” — Site Manager, MP Contractor
