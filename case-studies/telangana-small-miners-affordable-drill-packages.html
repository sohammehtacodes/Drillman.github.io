Telangana Small Miners — Affordable Drill Packages

Overview: Cluster of small miners needed cost-effective, safe drilling sets and guidance.

Challenge: High upfront costs and limited experience using modern rigs and pneumatic tools.

Solution: Bundled light-duty crawler, jackhammers, and starter spares; created simple SOPs and WhatsApp support.

Outcome: +14% average productivity, fewer misuse-related breakdowns, safer handling practices observed on audit.

Testimonial: “Affordable kits plus SOPs let us standardize quickly.” — Cooperative Lead, Telangana
